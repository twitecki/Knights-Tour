Welcome To The Knights Tour!
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Normal Mode - 
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The objective of this mode is to try to have the knight jump to every square on the board without jumping on 
the same square twice.
	-Start by clicking on any square to begin. The red circles represent the squares you have already been on. 
	 The green square represents the last square you jumped to.
	-Undo a move by pressing the 'u' key on the keyboard or scrolling backwards with the mouse. 
	-Redo a move by pressing the 'r' key on the keyboard or scrolling forwards with the mouse.
	-A hint option is available once you make 32 moves. It will give you a hint as to what square you should 
	 jump to next or if you are out of possible solutions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Adventure Mode -
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The objective of this mode is to make it through the 25 levels of the game.To complete a level, lay down flags 
from the starting flag square to the end square with legal chess knight moves. Watch out for the other pieces. 
Running into them will lose you a life. Run out of lives and you lose! If you die, you respawn on the last flag
you placed. You need to press the spacebar before you can start to move again. Once you respawn, you are invincible 
until you start moving again. Collect extra lives by placing a flag on a square with a heart on it. 
	
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Controls - 
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Arrow Keys - Move the knight around the board
'd' - Drop a flag on a square you are over top of 
's' - Snag a flag from a square you are over top of
'spacebar' - Start moving at the start of a new level, or after you respawn

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Good Luck!
